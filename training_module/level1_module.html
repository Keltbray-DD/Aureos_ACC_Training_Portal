Welcome to the Level 1 Essentials training

This training has been designed as a practical starting point for team members who are new to ACC or looking to build confidence with the platform's core features. Whether you’re on-site or in the office, this guide gives you the tools to begin working effectively within your project environment. It’s a great foundation before progressing to Level 2, which builds on these basics with more advanced tools. You don’t need to complete this guide in a specific order — use it when you need help with a particular task, or to explore more features in your own time.

What You’ll Find in This Guide

You’ll find simple, step-by-step instructions to help you:

  • Log in and access your project with ease
  • Navigate ACC modules confidently
  • Find, Share and View Files
  • Upload files using the correct process
  • Manage Project Emails

Logging In to ACC

Select an option below

How to Login

First go to the ACC hompage, then you will be redirected to the login page if signed out.

Once the login page has loaded enter your email that you have signed up to ACC with, this is normally your company email, then click Next.

For Aureos users you will be redirected to Micorsoft 365 as we have Single Sign On (SSO) enabled, please enter your Microsoft credentials to continue loggining in.

For non-Auroes users, this will depend on what configurations your company has setup, in most instances your will just login using the password you created when you first registered your account. If you can't remember please use the 'Forgot Password' option to reset it.

Once youve successfully logged in your will be redirected to the ACC Homepage, where you can see your Dashboard for items assgined to you or go to the projects tab to see the projects you have access to.
